# tomato chicken pasta ft baja chipotle suace from subway

hello and welcome to my tutorial to make tomato chicken pasta ft baja chiptole sauce from subway. you have to buy the suace at the store though.

first you need the ingreidneice (like i said you buy them at the store)

## incredienace
- breaded chicken filets (as many as you want but probably two)
- fire roasted diced tomatos (cannned)
- tomato sauce (canned)
- penne pasta (it can also be any pasta but maybe a chunky one is best for this)
- some cloves of garlic (to taste but do a good amount please) CHOPPED
- coconut milk if you're woke
- baja chipotle subway sauce
- lime juice
- secret: fresh oregano


## operation

for this tomato pasta you have to cook you pasta. do that

also start cooking you bread fried chicken filets. i do some that are pre-cooked but air fry from frozen and they come out so juicy and delicious so maybe try something like that for your pieaace of mind.

1. put the pan on the heat
2. put the olive oil (you need that also) in the pan
3. letting it warm
3. your chopped garlic goes in the oil and it sizzles on contact. listen to your food.
4. wait. they have to be golden brown and smell good.
5. open the diced tomatos and put it all in there. mix it around and then maybe add about half a can of the tomato suace. mix it more.
6. this is when i put in a drizzle of chipotle sauce or whatever it was. don't do too much, but this is to taste, but you can't do too much. but you can.
7. mix and incorportate that.
8. add about 2 tablespoons of coconut milk
9. mix and incorporate
10. you can add lime juice here if you want it.
11. do your timing so that your pata is ready now. put it in the sauce. you can put some pasta water in there too but you don't have to if you don't want.
12. mix it in there and maybe cover it so your pasta gets mixed in and it all becomes one (this might not happen also)
13. chop your chicken and put them in there if you want. 
14. mix and cook and mix it more so it's warm and juicey
15. do the same with the oregeano now you put it on there . mwah.

then when you are ready the time comes and you can be done with it.  it was really jummy for me idk about you.
