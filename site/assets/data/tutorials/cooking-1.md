# spam recipe

okay are you ready? so this time you will need:

## ingedience
- spam (cut it cubelike)
- garlic (chopped and ready)
- sesame oil
- oyster suace
- chili flakes (this can be lao gann ma or like anything idgaf)
- sugar (a little bit)
- coconut milk
- ramen just from a microwave ramen or whatever. you can ignore or embrace the fflavor packet in there.

## operation

1. first fry your spam cubes in a pan until it's golden brown and lushcious.
2. then you add in the garlic and get it beautiful and golden too
3. spill some sesame oil, oyster suace, chiliflakes, and a maybe tspoon of sugar on it
4. mix and incorporate and coat it all good.
5. make sure you were actually cooking your ramen this whole time.
6. take the ramen out of the noodle water and put it in the pan.
8. mix it in there and pour a little coconut milk and anything else on it.
9. that all mixes with your sauces already there and on the meats
10. don't do a lot of coconut milk though it's supposed to be almost like mac n cheese but reallynot that
