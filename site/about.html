about the site

this site is my very own WORM OS!!!!!! that is, worm operating system. i want this site to be an amusing space to visit, with educational and dare i say uplifting materials sprinkled around!

here is an example of my interactive pages! i'm not sure how i want to link this from my homepage just yet but i'll get there..

i think that a free internet is very important. and while making a personal site is a piece of fighting to de-monopolize social media, it misses the point. not everyone has the time/energy/interest/passion for creating and upholding a personal website, even a silly neocities. there's more to the issue than that: it's about how we engage with one another. the shut down of yesterweb has got me thinking about the importance of community.

that said, i sincerely want my site to encourage and foster interests in coding, webmastery, etc. it is empowering to create small digital spaces, personal or not. and by god digital literacy is important.

you can check my top nav for a link to the site's git repo, if you're interested.
